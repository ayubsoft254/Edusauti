Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does EduSauti support?

We currently support PDF, DOCX, and TXT files. We're working on adding support for more formats.

How does the Q&A feature work?

While listening to your audio summary, you can type questions about the content. Our AI will provide contextual answers in real-time, similar to asking questions in a classroom.

Is there a mobile app available?

We're currently focused on our web platform, but mobile apps for iOS and Android are on our roadmap for 2026.

How do I upgrade to a Pro account?

After creating an account, visit your account settings page where you'll find subscription options. You can upgrade to Pro with monthly or annual billing.
