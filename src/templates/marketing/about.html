About EduSauti

Making education accessible, engaging, and flexible for everyone through AI.

EduSauti was founded in 2025 with a vision to transform how people consume educational content. In a world where time is limited and learning styles vary, we recognized the need for a solution that adapts to the modern learner.

Our name, "EduSauti," combines "Education" with the Swahili word "Sauti," meaning "voice." This reflects our mission to give voice to educational content, making it more accessible and engaging for all.

Our Mission

To create a classroom-like learning experience that's available anywhere, anytime, for anyone. We believe that education should be accessible regardless of learning style, physical ability, or time constraints.

Our Values

  • Accessibility - Making education available to everyone, regardless of learning style or physical ability
  • Innovation - Constantly improving our technology to provide the best possible audio learning experience
  • Quality - Ensuring natural, engaging voice synthesis that makes learning enjoyable
  • Community - Building a community of lifelong learners who support each other

Our Technology

EduSauti leverages cutting-edge Azure AI services to provide a seamless learning experience:

  • Azure Document Intelligence - Extracts structured text from uploaded documents
  • Azure OpenAI - Creates educational summaries and powers our Q&A system
  • Azure Speech Services - Converts text to natural-sounding speech
  • Django Channels - Enables real-time Q&A interaction

We're committed to continuous improvement and regularly update our platform based on user feedback and technological advancements.

Our Roadmap

We've just launched our initial version, but we have big plans for the future:

  • Expanding into mobile (Android & iOS)
  • Adding multilingual support (Swahili, French, Arabic, etc.)
  • Enhanced analytics for tracking learning progress
  • API development for integration with LMS platforms
