About EduSauti

Making education accessible, engaging, and flexible for everyone through AI.

EduSauti was founded in 2025 with a vision to transform how people consume educational content. In a world where time is limited and learning styles vary, we recognized the need for a solution that adapts to the modern learner.

Our name, "EduSauti," combines "Education" with the Swahili word "Sauti," meaning "voice." This reflects our mission to give voice to educational content, making it more accessible and engaging for all.

Our Mission

To create a classroom-like learning experience that's available anywhere, anytime, for anyone. We believe that education should be accessible regardless of learning style, physical ability, or time constraints.

Our Technology

Azure Document Intelligence

Our platform uses Azure Document Intelligence to extract structured text from various document formats including PDFs, Word documents, and plain text files.

This advanced service accurately captures the content structure, maintaining the semantic organization of your educational materials for better comprehension.

Azure OpenAI

We leverage Azure OpenAI to create educational summaries that are structured like a teacher would present them, highlighting key concepts and connections.

The same powerful AI technology powers our real-time question answering system, providing context-aware responses about the document content while you're listening.

Azure Speech Services

Our platform uses Azure Speech Services to convert text into natural-sounding audio with proper pacing, emphasis, and intonation.

Users can choose from multiple voice options and adjust playback speed to match their learning preferences, creating a personalized listening experience.

Real-time Q&A with Django Channels

EduSauti uses Django Channels and WebSockets to create a real-time, interactive learning experience where you can ask questions while listening.

This mimics the classroom experience of raising your hand to ask a question, but available anywhere and anytime you're learning.
